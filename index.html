About

Hey there, I'm Emmanuel, a software Development Engineer intern at Amazon. I revel in solving critical problems, developing algorithms, and building cutting-edge applications. I thrive in fast-paced environments and enjoy working with others towards success as a team.


I trained to be a software engineer because I just love to code. Growing up as kid, I never had the luxury to own a computer but was really fascinated about how it worked. Everytime my uncle comes over, I spend alot of time within a short time frame to to learn all I can be he leaves. I got started with a simple objective; I would like to built my own apps and websites, little did I know I was going to be pursing a dream and career in web development and design.

I previously worked on a Robotics Project at a Funded National Science Foundation (NSF) project at TSU. As a researcher and engineer I bulit and launched a Robotics web app. I've built websites for Non profit organizations like the People Family workshop and worked a TA for the computer science department at TSU. In my own time i work on Open Source projects on GitHub. I have Computer Science degree from Texas Southern University.
